The shape of nearness

A graph normally draws people as identical dots and connections as identical lines. But real people aren't identical — and neither is what happens to you when one of them gets close.

Drag the circles. Watch what happens.

The patterns inside each circle are personality, fixed. The size, the brightness, the thickness of the lines — those move with the company you keep.
